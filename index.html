Privacy Policy

PropTech AI Time Management (Chrome Extension) — Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Chrome extension ("the extension") is built and operated by Proptech AI LLC ("we", "us", "the company") solely for use by our own employees, as an internal workplace time-tracking tool. It is not available to the public and is not intended for use by anyone other than our current employees who have been issued a company account for this purpose.

By installing and signing in to this extension, an employee acknowledges that their work activity, as described below, is recorded for payroll and internal productivity/compliance purposes as a condition of their employment.

What the extension collects

  • Account information — the employee's name and email address, used to identify their tracked time in our internal system.
  • Work session timestamps — when tracking starts and stops, and periods of idle time (detected via mouse/keyboard inactivity) and breaks.
  • Activity indicators — counts of mouse and keyboard events per minute while actively tracking, used only to measure activity level. We do not log keystroke content, typed text, or which keys are pressed — only an event count.
  • Active tab domain — the domain (e.g. example.com) of whichever browser tab is in focus while tracking is active, used to determine whether the employee is on an approved work-related site. We do not collect full browsing history, page content, or activity outside of active tracking sessions.
  • Periodic screenshots — a small number of screenshots are captured at randomized points during a tracked session, for internal productivity/compliance review by the employee's employer.
  • Self-reported lead counts — for employees in sales-related roles, a count of leads they submit for that day.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell any collected data to third parties.
  • We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or information resellers.
  • We do not use this data for advertising, profiling unrelated to work performance, or to determine creditworthiness or lending eligibility.
  • We do not collect data outside of an active, employee-initiated tracking session.
  • The extension does not read, log, or transmit passwords or content typed into other websites — only the domain name of the active tab.

Where data is stored and who can see it

All data collected by the extension is transmitted securely (HTTPS) and stored in our company's own private Supabase project — infrastructure we control, not a third-party analytics or advertising service. It is visible only to authorized personnel within Proptech AI LLC (administrators and, for specific tabs, designated internal reviewer roles) through our internal admin dashboard, for the payroll and operational purposes described above.

Data retention and deletion

Tracked time data is retained for as long as needed for payroll and business records. Screenshots are periodically purged after a bounded retention window. If an employee's account is removed, their associated tracked data and screenshots are deleted as part of that offboarding process.

Employee rights

Current or former employees with questions about data collected by this extension, or requests regarding that data, should contact their manager or HR directly.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and, where required, notice will be given to employees before continued use of the extension.
